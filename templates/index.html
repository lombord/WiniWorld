Welcome to WiniWorld!

  • Web App Audience

    People willing to play their favorite mini games with their friends or compete with random people and just have fun

  • Functionality

    Main functionality of the Web App is being able to play popular mini games with other people and compete with them to get scores.People will be able to have their personal profiles where they can customize their names, avatars, info etc.They will be able to create their own rooms for certain games to play with only allowed members.

  • Content distribution

    When u visit Web App for a first time u will have to login or signup.Once you have done that u will be able to see main page where you can choose what to play. Each game will be as a separate app, have it's own pages and it's own top players list.Each time when u win somebody u get scores and coins for which u can buy skins or other special items.And Every game is gonna have it's own live chat where players can talk.
